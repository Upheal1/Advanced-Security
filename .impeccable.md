## Design Context

### Users
People using a mental-health gamification app (primary users likely teens/adults, with caregivers indirectly involved).

### Brand Personality
Calm, encouraging, and gently playful; supportive when users feel overwhelmed, and rewarding when they show progress.

### Aesthetic Direction
Soft, modern UI with rounded cards, subtle gradients, and a restricted accent palette driven by app tokens (purple/teal/orange).

### Design Principles
1. Strong visual hierarchy within ~2 seconds (clear sectioning and one obvious primary action).
2. Legibility first: avoid overly small typography; keep labels comfortably readable.
3. Strategic touch affordances: interactive surfaces use `InkWell`-style feedback (ripple, proper tap targets).
4. Token-first styling: prefer `AppColors` (and theme brightness) over one-off hard-coded colors.
5. Quiet motion: animations should be subtle and purpose-driven.
